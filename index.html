About

Prophet Plant is a research and therapy center focused on the safe, guided, and transformative use of psychedelics—including Cannabis, Psilocybin, and N,N-DMT—for deep healing, consciousness expansion, and scientific exploration.

At the beginning of each session, a carefully curated soundscape featuring the instruments and healing frequencies used in the retreat will be played through speakers. These immersive sounds set the energetic foundation for deep relaxation, alignment, and connection to the healing process.
